Wireframe c|net website

My wireframe of the c|net website homepage. One of my top 3 websites for visual design and usability.

What problem is the web page solving?

How to display a variety of content in a way that is visually appealing while still displaying the ads to optimize for clicks. It must do this while communicating to the viewer that more content exists "below the fold." The viewer needs to be aware of and able to access a wide array of options without feeling overwhelmed which will result in paralysis.

I believe that c|net was highly successful at solving this problem and dealing with the visual challenges listed below. That is precisely why I choose this as one of my top 3 favorite sites

 

What visual challenges are there?

  • Need to display the c|net logo in a way that people will notice and recognize it without making it so large that it distracts from the content.
  • How to display visually stunning images for blog entries in a uniform way so they can be plugged in like a module and still keep the site from looking cluttered.
  • Draw attention to the ads without making it obvious that we are trying to sell something or otherwise detracting from the content.
  • How to communicate that there is more content on the page if you scroll down.
